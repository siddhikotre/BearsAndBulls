How to play?

Guess the word in minimum tries

  • The server will choose a random word which will not be visible to the player.
  • The player can type the word he wants to play in the text box and then click on the ENTER button.

  • a. If your word and the word selected by the server have common letters but at different positions, the count of such letters will be the number of bears.

  • b. If your word and the word selected by the server have common letters and at the same positions, the count of such letters will be the number of bulls.

  • FOR EXAMPLE: The sever generated SUPERWORD is:
    • S H O E
  • The first word you have guessed is:
    • D O M E
  • Since they have common letter 'O' and same position for 'E':
    • S H O E
    D O M E
  • Thus we have 1 Bear and 1 Bull
  • On guessing all four letters of words at the correct position, you win the game.
  • To reset a game to get a new word from the server, press the RESET button.
